Chungking Express, by Wong Kar-wai

When I saw this film I really wanted to see it because I had heard and read a lot about it. I had previously enjoyed Fallen Angels and Happy Together and this time was no exception. It is about the characters' attempts to love and their need to not feel alone. The aesthetics of his world is of a melancholic simplicity, brilliant and deeply human. I will say no more but I highly recommend watching this movie.
